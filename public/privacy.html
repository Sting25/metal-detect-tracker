Privacy & Data Security

We built Signal Bouncer because we're detectorists too. We understand that your site locations and finds are valuable — and private. Here's exactly how we protect your data.

Your Data Stays Yours

  • We never sell, share, or give your data to third parties.
  • Other users cannot see your sites, finds, or GPS coordinates — ever.
  • We don't run ads and don't use tracking for advertising purposes.
  • You can export all your data (sites, finds, photos) as a ZIP file anytime from Settings.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

GPS & Location Privacy

  • You control whether exact GPS coordinates are stored — you can round them to ~1 km in Settings.
  • When exporting data, you can choose to round or completely remove coordinates.
  • GPS data is stored only on our server and is never sent anywhere else.
  • We do not track your location in the background. GPS is only used when you choose to log a find or start a hunt.

Security

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt — we never store or see your password.
  • We support passkey authentication for passwordless, phishing-resistant sign-in.
  • Session tokens are short-lived and securely managed.

Site Sharing

  • Sharing is always opt-in. You choose exactly which sites to share and with whom.
  • You can revoke access to shared sites at any time.
  • Nothing you add to Signal Bouncer is ever publicly visible.

Analytics

  • We use minimal analytics (Google Analytics) to understand how the app is used — not to track individuals.
  • We do not fingerprint browsers or create user profiles for marketing.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, use the Feedback button in the app or contact the admin.
